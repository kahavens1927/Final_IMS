The Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Speedway, Indiana is home to the single largest sporting event on the planet, the Indianapolis 500. Hosting over 350,000 people from countless countries across the globe, it is a spectacle only to be seen in person. I have been to over 20 races in my life and hope to continue the tradition until I can no longer go. Memorial Day Weekend holds a special place in a lot of people's heart for this event and I believe everyone should attend at least once.

Kyle Havens 2024
